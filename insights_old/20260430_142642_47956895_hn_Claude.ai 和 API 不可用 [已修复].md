## 主要洞察

### 1. Claude 可靠性危机

- **单九可靠��**：Claude 目前仅有约 90%（即"single 9"）的正常运行时间，用户估计 uptime 约 98.68%
- **频繁宕机**：多位用户反映 Claude 经常无预警宕机，影响工作效率
- **EndStreamResponse 错误**：部分用户在 Claude Code 中遇到 `[unknown] missing EndStreamResponse` 错误

> "This is a tough moment. Claude is simultaneously becoming substantially more expensive, substantially less reliable (single 9 of reliability), and substantially less performant." — aliljet

### 2. 用户大规模转向 Codex/GPT-5.5

大量用户表示已切换或考虑切换到 OpenAI Codex 和 GPT-5.5：

- **Token 消耗更低**：有用户反映切换后每月节省约 $100
- **稳定性更好**：Codex 被描述为"ambient and reliable"，会话可维持数天不断开
- **实际使用体验**：用户报告 Codex"just works and does what I ask"、不中断、结果直接可用

> "Plus one. I held off for too long... I switched a few days ago and work has been much less frustrating. Feels like CC did back in February before they started playing games." — stldev

### 3. Claude 模型质量问题

- **Claude 4.7 被批评**：被形容为"lazy"、不愿承担责任、喜欢猜测而假设自己正确
- **"傻瓜式"变化**：从 4.5 到 4.7 的体验变化被比喻为"从听话的初级工程师变成了自以为是的中级工程师"

> "4.5 -> 4.7 feels like going from working with a coach-able, junior engineer... to working with a cocky mid-level that will spend too long on pointless tangents." — o10449366

### 4. AI 模型商品化趋势

社区认为当前 AI 模型已接近同质化，可靠性成为核心竞争力：

> "I think we've hit the 'who's the most reliable' stage for these tools. We can now shop around easily. They almost all do the same thing now. The models are 'Just Enough'." — etoxin

### 5. Claude vs Codex 混合使用策略

部分高级用户采���双工具策略：

- **Claude 优势**：更好的架构/设计/规划能力
